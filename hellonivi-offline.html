How It Works

Nivi learns your voice from writing samples and interview answers. Every day, she:

  1. Scouts trending posts from your target creators
  2. Drafts voice-matched content in your style
  3. Sends it to WhatsApp for approval
  4. Publishes to LinkedIn with one word
  5. Tracks performance analytics in real-time
